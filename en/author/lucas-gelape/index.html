Lucas Gelape

Political Scientist and Data Analyst

Political scientist and data analyst. My work focuses on political institutions and I have experience with research design, and data analysis of political, socioeconomic and social media data.

PhD Candidate, Political Science, University of São Paulo. Visiting fellow at Harvard University (09/2019 to 02/2020). MA in Political Science (2017) and Bachelor of Laws (2014), Federal University of Minas Gerais.

I contribute to Volt Data Lab’s projects as a data analyst. Worked as a data scientist on G1 between July and November, 2018, where I produced news about the Brazilian general elections.

In my website, you can access a blog about works I have been involved with, find information on developed softwares (R packages), my academic research, courses taught and teaching assistant positions.
